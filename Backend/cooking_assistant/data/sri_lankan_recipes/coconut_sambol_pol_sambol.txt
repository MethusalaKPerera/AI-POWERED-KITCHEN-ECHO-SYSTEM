Coconut Sambol (Pol Sambol)
පොල් සම්බෝල
தேங்காய் சம்பல்
======================================================================

Category: Sambol
Region: General
Difficulty: Easy
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 0 minutes
Servings: 6

Description:
Spicy coconut condiment with chili, onions, and lime

INGREDIENTS:
----------------------------------------
• 2 cups freshly grated coconut
• 1 large onion, finely chopped
• 2 tbsp chili powder (or to taste)
• 1 tsp salt
• 2 tbsp lime juice
• 1 tsp Maldive fish (optional)
• Few curry leaves, chopped

INSTRUCTIONS:
----------------------------------------
1. In a large bowl, combine grated coconut and salt
2. Add chili powder and mix well
3. Add finely chopped onions
4. Add Maldive fish if using
5. Add chopped curry leaves
6. Squeeze lime juice over the mixture
7. Mix everything thoroughly with your hands
8. Adjust seasoning to taste
9. Serve immediately or store in refrigerator

Cultural Notes:
Pol sambol is the most essential Sri Lankan condiment, served with every meal

TIPS:
• Use fresh coconut for best taste
• Can be stored for 2-3 days refrigerated

Chicken Curry (Kukul Mas Curry)
කුකුල් මස් කරිය
கோழி கறி
======================================================================

Category: Curry
Region: Sinhalese
Difficulty: Medium
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 6

Description:
A rich, aromatic chicken curry with roasted Sri Lankan spices and coconut milk

INGREDIENTS:
----------------------------------------
• 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
• 400ml thick coconut milk
• 200ml thin coconut milk
• 3 tbsp roasted curry powder
• 1 tbsp chili powder
• 1 tsp turmeric powder
• 2 large onions, sliced
• 4 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 inch ginger, minced
• 2-3 green chilies
• 1 sprig curry leaves
• 1 stick cinnamon
• 3 cardamom pods
• 4 cloves
• 2 tbsp vegetable oil
• Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS:
----------------------------------------
1. Marinate chicken with turmeric, chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes
2. Heat oil in a large pan and add cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves
3. Add curry leaves and sauté until fragrant
4. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown
5. Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies; sauté for 2 minutes
6. Add roasted curry powder and cook for 1 minute
7. Add marinated chicken and mix well
8. Pour in thin coconut milk and bring to a boil
9. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is tender
10. Add thick coconut milk and simmer for 10 more minutes
11. Adjust seasoning and serve hot with rice

Cultural Notes:
This is the most popular Sri Lankan chicken curry, typically served at special occasions and family gatherings

TIPS:
• Use freshly roasted curry powder for best flavor
• Marinating overnight enhances the taste

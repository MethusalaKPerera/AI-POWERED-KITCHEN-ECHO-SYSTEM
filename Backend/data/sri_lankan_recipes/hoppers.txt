Hoppers (Appa)
Ingredients:

2 cups rice flour
1/2 cup coconut milk
1/2 teaspoon yeast
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup water
Coconut oil for pan

Instructions:

Mix yeast with sugar and warm water
Add rice flour, coconut milk, and salt
Mix to smooth batter
Let ferment for 3-4 hours
Heat hopper pan with oil
Pour batter in circular motion
Cover and cook until edges are crispy
Serve with curry or sambol

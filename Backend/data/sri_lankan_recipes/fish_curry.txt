Sri Lankan Fish Curry
Ingredients:

500g fish (tuna or mackerel)
2 onions, sliced
3 cloves garlic
1 inch ginger
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 cup coconut milk
2 tablespoons curry powder
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon tamarind paste
Curry leaves
2 green chilies
Salt to taste

Instructions:

Marinate fish with turmeric and salt
Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger
Add curry powder and tomatoes
Add fish pieces gently
Add coconut milk and tamarind
Add curry leaves and green chilies
Simmer for 15-20 minutes
Serve with rice

Sri Lankan Rice and Curry (Basic Meal)
Components:

Steamed white rice
Main curry (chicken, fish, or dhal)
Vegetable curry (beans, carrots, or pumpkin)
Pol sambol (coconut sambol)
Papadam (crispy lentil wafer)
Pickle or chutney

Key Ingredients Used:

Rice
Coconut milk
Curry leaves
Chili powder
Turmeric
Onions
Garlic
Ginger

Typical Spices:

Cinnamon
Cardamom
Cumin
Coriander
Mustard seeds

This is the traditional Sri Lankan meal served on a banana leaf or plate.

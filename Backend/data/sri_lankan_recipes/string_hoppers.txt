String Hoppers (Idiyappam)
Ingredients:

2 cups rice flour
2 cups water
1 teaspoon salt
Coconut oil for greasing

Instructions:

Boil water with salt
Add rice flour gradually while stirring
Knead into a soft dough
Fill string hopper press with dough
Steam in circular motions onto greased plates
Steam for 5-7 minutes
Serve hot with curry and sambol

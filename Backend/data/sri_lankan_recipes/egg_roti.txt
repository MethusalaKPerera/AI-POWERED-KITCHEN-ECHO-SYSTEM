Egg Roti
Ingredients:

2 cups wheat flour
3 eggs
1 onion, finely chopped
2 green chilies, chopped
Curry leaves
1/2 teaspoon salt
Water as needed
Oil for cooking

Instructions:

Mix flour with salt and water to make dough
Let dough rest for 30 minutes
Beat eggs with onions, chilies, and curry leaves
Roll dough into thin circles
Pour egg mixture on top of rolled dough
Fold edges and seal
Cook on griddle with oil until golden
Serve hot with curry or sambol

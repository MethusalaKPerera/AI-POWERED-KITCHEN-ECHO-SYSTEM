Sri Lankan Dhal Curry

Ingredients:
- 1 cup red lentils
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 1 cup coconut milk

Instructions:
Boil lentils with turmeric. Temper curry leaves. Add onions and garlic. Mix with lentils and coconut milk. Simmer 10 minutes.

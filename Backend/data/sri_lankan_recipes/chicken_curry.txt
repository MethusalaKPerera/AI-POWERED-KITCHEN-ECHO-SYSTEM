Sri Lankan Chicken Curry

Ingredients:
- 1 kg chicken pieces
- 2 large onions, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, minced
- 2 green chilies, sliced
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 2 tablespoons oil
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
Heat oil and saut√© onions until golden. Add garlic, ginger, chilies. Add spices. Add chicken and coconut milk. Simmer 40 minutes. Serve with rice.
